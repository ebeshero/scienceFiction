Context:

  • Orson Welles’ Radio Broadcast, 1938
  • During the 1930s actors Orson Welles and John Houseman presented a radio show, The Mercury Theatre on the Air. This show is remembered most for its October 30, 1938 broadcast of The War of the Worlds . Although listeners were cautioned that this was only a story, panic ensued across the country as people thought it was a real news broadcast and that the earth was being invaded by Martians.
